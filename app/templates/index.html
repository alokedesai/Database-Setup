Apployment helps you find college developers and designers from some of the nation's top colleges to do freelance work for you. Right now, we only allow students from top 20 schools so that you can ensure you're getting a quality freelancer.

Often, it's not necessary to pay development firms thousands of dollars to get something built that a college student could do just as well. College students tend to be up to 60% cheaper than professional firms.

A lot of freelancing jobs end up getting sent out of the country, especially on sites like Freelancer and Elance. Our goal is to keep as many jobs in America as possible by providing students who are competitively priced with those that are overseas.
